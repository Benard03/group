Registration

A customer without an account will have to register and create one ,using their email adress ,password and later confirm their passwords by re-entering it. On the other hand,a client with an already existing account will have to directly login or signin without re-registering on the app.

Identify User Category

After a user has logined in ,one has to choose the user category he/she falls under ,if its a manager,tap manager,if its a client ,tap client and follow the prompts

Menu Management

Upload your own dishes, cuisines, and attract your customers with an attractive menu.

With an attractive food menu, help your customers choose their orders easily. From different food categories to exciting food pictures, give your customers well-crafted and structured menus to order food online.

Placing an order

Let your customers add their favorite dishes to cart, re-order, or schedule their orders easily.

With an easy and user-friendly functionality, your customers can add their favorite dishes to cart, schedule their orders beforehand to ensure timely delivery or re-order if they want to order again.

Confirm your order

Here the customers,will have to fully confirm their orders by generating a QR code that collects data or information about all the ordered items.This QR code is scanned at pick-up time at the restaurant.

Manager's page

Manager is able to keep track of the orders made on a daily basis and the total cost /income got on a particular day.
